About Me.

As I became familiar with the basic skills needed to create a webpage, I found enjoyment in what I was learning. The decision to pursue coding and make it my career was quickly made, and it was not long before I became confident in HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Today, I work as a Software Engineer Apprentice in London, England. I am loving every step of my journey.
